Why LNMIIT?


The ​LNM Institute of Information Technology (LNMIIT), ​Jaipur is a joint venture of the Government of Rajasthan and the ​LUM (Lakshmi Niwas Mittal & Usha Mittal) Foundation headed by the world steel baron ​Mr. Lakshmi Niwas Mittal (Chairman Arcelor-Mittal). This institute has been serving the industry for over a decade.The institute is at par with all the NITs and IIITs when it comes to admissions. The students are admitted on the basis of JEE Mains scores and the cut offs are always higher than national qualifying score. Last year’s placement drive saw the arrival of some of the most sought-after companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe, Sprinkler, Zomato, Samsung to name a few. Research internships were also offered to the students.

Recruitment Procedure

    The process involves the following steps
  • Companies share the filled job announcement form (JAF) and JD.
  • After the Placement Cell is okay with the proposal slot decided and discussed by the company.
  • JAF is shared with students and interested students register for company as per the eligibility decided by the company.
  • Data is discussed with the company to process further.
  • Date is mutually decided by the college and company for an on campus event.
